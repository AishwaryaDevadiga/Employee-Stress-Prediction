## 🔄 Data Flow

```
Step 1: Load
  dataset/company_employee_details4999.csv
    ↓
Step 2: Clean (preprocessing.py)
  Remove duplicates, handle missing values
    ↓
Step 3: Encode (preprocessing.py)
  Gender and categorical columns
    ↓
Step 4: Engineer Features (feature_engineering.py)
  Add 5 calculated features
    ↓
Step 5: Save (outputs/processed_data.csv)
  4,981 × 23 clean dataset
    ↓
Step 6: Train Models (model_trainer.py)
  4 algorithms on general data
    ↓
Step 7: Train Gender Models (gender_specific_models.py)
  4 algorithms per gender (8 total)
    ↓
Step 8: Save Models (models/*.pkl)
  All trained models
    ↓
Step 9: Dashboard (dashboard.py)
  Load models, make predictions, show results
```
